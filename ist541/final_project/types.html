Silken Tofu

This type of tofu has the highest water content. It falls apart easily and has a texture similar to burrata.

Soft Tofu

This type is primarily used in Asian dishes. Soft tofu easily soaks up high levels of flavor and is often used in soups and stews.

Firm Tofu

This is the type commonly found in supermarkets. It is effortless to chop and the most versatile to cook.

Extra Firm Tofu

Extra firm tofu has less water content than firm, but it does not take in marinades well.
